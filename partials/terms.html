2. Our service and status

IVA claims (FCA-regulated): We provide an initial assessment of potential IVA mis-selling and, with your consent, may introduce you to an appropriate SRA-regulated solicitor for legal advice.

Business energy claims (unregulated): We provide an initial assessment of potential business energy mis-selling and, with your consent, may introduce you to a specialist commercial solicitor. This service is not regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority.

We do not provide legal advice; any legal advice is given solely by the appointed solicitor. You are free to choose another solicitor at any time.

7. Complaints

If you are unhappy with our service, please contact support@visiblelegal.co.uk. We will acknowledge your complaint promptly and aim to provide our final response within 8 weeks.

For IVA claims (FCA-regulated): If you remain dissatisfied, or if we do not respond within 8 weeks, you can refer your complaint to the Financial Ombudsman Service free of charge within 6 months of our final response: Financial Ombudsman Service, Exchange Tower, London E14 9SR. Telephone: 0800 023 4567 or 0300 123 9123.

For business energy claims (unregulated): As this is not an FCA-regulated service, the Financial Ombudsman Service complaints route does not apply. If we cannot resolve your complaint, you may seek independent legal advice or contact your local Trading Standards service.

If your complaint relates to legal services provided by a solicitor, please use that firm's complaints procedure. You may have rights to escalate to the Legal Ombudsman; the solicitor will explain this in their documentation.
