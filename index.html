What We Do

Visible Legal Marketing Ltd is a claims management company. We help people who may have been mis-sold financial products by assessing their case and, where appropriate, connecting them with specialist solicitors who can pursue a legal claim on their behalf.

For IVA mis-selling claims, we obtain your IVA file from your provider through a Data Subject Access Request, assess whether the advice you received was suitable, and determine whether you have a viable claim. If we identify potential mis-selling, we refer your case to a panel solicitor who handles the legal claim.

For business energy claims, we review your energy contracts to identify undisclosed broker commissions and, where appropriate, refer your case to specialist solicitors for recovery.

Fees and Costs

There are no upfront costs to you at any stage. Visible Legal Marketing Ltd carries out initial work on your behalf at no charge, including obtaining your case file and assessing whether you have a viable claim.

If we identify a viable claim, we refer your case to a panel solicitor. The solicitor handles the legal claim and their fees are set out in their own client care letter, regulated by the Solicitors Regulation Authority. Solicitor fees are typically between 18 and 36 percent (including VAT) of any compensation recovered, and are only charged if the claim is successful.

Visible Legal Marketing Ltd receives a fixed referral fee from the solicitor for the assessment and referral work we carry out. This fee is paid by the solicitor and does not reduce or affect any compensation you receive.

Your Rights

You do not need to use a claims management company to make your complaint. You can complain for free directly to your IVA provider, energy broker, or lender. If you are not satisfied with their response, you can escalate your complaint to the Financial Ombudsman Service free of charge.

Many clients choose to work with us because we manage the process, obtain documentation, assess the case, and coordinate with solicitors on their behalf.
