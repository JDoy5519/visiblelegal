What We Do

Visible Legal Marketing is a claims management company. Here is what that means for you.

We obtain your documents

We submit formal requests to your provider for the documents needed to assess your case. You sign a Letter of Authority and we handle the rest.

We assess your case

We review your documents and determine whether you have a viable claim. We look at the advice you received, the alternatives available, and the costs involved.

We connect you with solicitors

If we identify a viable claim, we refer your case to a specialist solicitor from our panel who pursues the legal claim on your behalf.

How We Work With You

No upfront costs

We carry out initial work on your behalf at no charge. If your case is referred to a solicitor, their fees are set out in their own client care letter.

You approve everything

You see and approve important documents before they are used. Nothing happens without your knowledge and consent.

We explain our fees clearly

Solicitor fees (typically 18 to 36 percent including VAT) are only charged on successful claims. We receive a fixed referral fee from the solicitor, not from you.

You can complain without us

You are free to complain directly to your provider or the Financial Ombudsman Service at any time, free of charge. A claims management company is not required.
