What happens next

How the claims process works

A straightforward process. You share your details once, then we coordinate with your provider and our solicitors and keep you updated at key stages. Financial claims follow FCA-regulated procedures; business energy claims follow established commercial practice.

  1. 1

    Complete our online form

    You answer a short set of questions about your claim. This helps us understand your situation and whether there may be grounds for a complaint.

  2. 2

    We verify details and request information

    We check the information you’ve provided and, where appropriate, request documents from your IVA provider, lender or bank (for example, agreements, statements and account notes) so the full picture can be reviewed.

  3. 3

    A specialist solicitor reviews your case

    Once the documents are received, a specialist UK solicitor from our panel reviews your case in detail. If they believe there is a strong basis for complaint, they will prepare or approve the complaint and any supporting arguments.

  4. 4

    Complaint outcome and next steps

    Your provider responds to the complaint. If successful, compensation or other redress may be offered. If not, the solicitor can advise you on possible next steps, including whether it may be appropriate to escalate to the Financial Ombudsman Service.

Who we are

About Visible Legal Marketing

Visible Legal Marketing is a claims management company, FCA-regulated for financial mis-selling and also offering unregulated business energy claim support. We help clients understand whether their IVA or business energy contract may have been mis-sold and coordinate the process with specialist UK solicitors where appropriate.

Focused on clarity, not confusion

Financial products are often taken out during difficult moments — debt pressure, limited advice, rushed conversations or unclear explanations. That’s where problems start. Our role is to slow everything down, review what actually happened and give you a clear, honest view of your options.

We specialise in a small number of claim types — IVAs and business energy — because depth matters. By staying close to these areas we track lender behaviour, regulatory changes, trends in mis-selling and the real outcomes solicitors are seeing day-to-day. That lets us give you realistic expectations rather than vague promises.

  • Focused: We don’t chase every possible claim. We operate where we understand the landscape deeply.
  • Evidence-led: We gather the documents, understand the timeline and build a clear picture before anything is submitted.
  • Client-first: No pressure. If a complaint isn’t realistic or worthwhile, we’ll tell you immediately.
  • Clear communication: You know what’s been requested, what’s outstanding, and what happens next.

From your first form submission to any solicitor involvement, the process stays structured and transparent. You’ll know who’s handling your case, why certain documents are needed and how close you are to the next stage at every step.

How we operate

  • Regulated where required: We are authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority for IVA claims management and must follow strict rules for those services. Business energy claims are not FCA-regulated but follow established professional standards.

  • Our role: We gather your initial information, help coordinate requests for documents from your provider and keep you updated at key milestones while the solicitor handles the legal work.
  • Fees: There is no upfront cost for using our service. If your claim succeeds and you receive compensation, a success fee is deducted, typically 18–36% (including VAT) depending on the case.
